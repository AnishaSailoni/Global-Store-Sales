🌍 Global Store Sales Performance Dashboard

Project Title / Headline

📊 Global Store Sales Performance & Product Insights Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze regional sales performance, product profitability, monthly sales trends, and country-wise business contribution for a global retail store business.


1. Short Description / Purpose

The Global Store Sales Dashboard is a dynamic Power BI report created to monitor sales, profit, and order performance across multiple regions and product categories. The dashboard helps businesses identify top-performing regions, profitable product categories, sales trends over time, and opportunities for business improvement.


2. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

* 📊 Power BI Desktop – Used for designing and developing the dashboard report.
* 📂 Power Query – Used for data transformation, cleaning, and preprocessing.
* 🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used for creating KPIs, calculated measures, profit metrics, and aggregations.
* 📝 Data Modeling – Used to establish relationships between tables and enable accurate filtering.
* 📁 File Formats – `.pbix` for Power BI report development and `.png` for dashboard preview images.


3. Data Source

Dataset Name:Global Store Sales Dataset
Source Type:Retail & Sales Transaction Dataset

The dataset contains transactional and sales-related information including:

* Sales Amount
* Profit
* Order Count
* Product Name
* Product Category
* Region
* Country
* Order Date
* Customer Purchase Data
* Monthly Sales Trends

The dataset supports analysis of sales performance, regional contribution, product demand, and profitability insights across different countries and categories.


4. Features / Highlights

 Business Problem

Global retail businesses handle large volumes of sales and operational data. Without proper visualization, it becomes difficult to answer critical business questions such as:

* Which region generates the highest sales?
* Which product categories perform best?
* How do sales fluctuate throughout the year?
* Which countries contribute the most to total sales?
* Which products generate high sales but lower profits?
* How can business performance be improved strategically?


🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

The goal of this dashboard is to provide an interactive platform for stakeholders to:

* Monitor overall sales and profitability
* Analyze region-wise business performance
* Track monthly sales trends
* Identify high-performing products and categories
* Support pricing and operational decisions
* Improve strategic business planning


📊 Walkthrough of Key Visuals

 1. KPI Cards (Top Section)

The dashboard displays key business metrics including:

* 💰 Total Sales
* 📈 Total Profit
* 📦 Total Orders

These KPIs provide a quick overview of overall business performance.


2. Key Insights & Recommendations Panel

📌 A dedicated insights section summarizes important findings such as:

Key Insights

* Central region generates the highest sales
* Technology category performs best
* Sales peak toward the year-end
* Some products have high sales but low profitability

Recommendations

* Focus on high-profit categories like Technology
* Improve low-performing regions
* Optimize pricing for low-margin products

This section improves executive-level decision-making.


 3. Sales Trend by Region (Bar Chart)

📌 Displays total sales contribution by region.

Insights:

* Helps identify high-performing and low-performing regions
* Useful for regional expansion and marketing strategy


 4. Sales Trend by Month (Line Chart)

📌 Shows monthly sales fluctuations throughout the year.

 Insights:

* Identifies seasonal sales patterns
* Detects peak business months
* Supports future sales forecasting


5. Sales & Profit by Country (Waterfall Chart)

📌 Displays country-wise contribution toward total sales and profit.

Insights:

* Highlights countries contributing significantly to business growth
* Useful for international business strategy and investment planning


6. Top Products by Sales (Bar Chart)

📌 Shows the highest-selling products based on sales amount.

 Insights:

* Helps identify popular products
* Supports inventory and product strategy decisions
* Detects products with strong sales demand


7. Interactive Slicers (Left Panel)

Users can dynamically filter the dashboard using:

* Region
* Category
* Order Date

These slicers improve interactivity and allow customized analysis.

7. How to Use the Dashboard

1. Open the `.pbix` file using Power BI Desktop.
2. Use slicers to filter data by region, category, and order date.
3. Review KPI cards for overall business metrics.
4. Analyze regional and monthly sales trends using charts.
5. Explore product and country-level insights for detailed business analysis.
6. Interact with visuals for deeper drill-down insights.
